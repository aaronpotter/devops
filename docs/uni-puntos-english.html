Frequently Asked Questions

What benefits will I enjoy if I apply and am approved for the Univision MasterCard Credit Card?

Once you are approved, you will receive the Univision MasterCard Credit Card in 7-10 business days. Once you receive your card you can immediately start using your card for purchases where MasterCard is accepted. Here are some of the benefits you can enjoy with your Univision MasterCard Credit Card:

  • Earn 2 points per $1 on gas and grocery purchases
  • Earn 1 point per $1 on all on all other purchases
  • Zero fraud liability on all unauthorized purchases
  • Ability to redeem reward points for gift cards, merchandise and unique Univision experiences

Can I make a same-day payment online?

Yes, online payments submitted before 6pm Eastern Time (ET) can be credited to your account today. Online payments submitted after 6pm ET will be credited the following day (or on your future scheduled payment date). Please note that it may take up to two business days to complete the financial transfer from your personal bank account to your Univision MasterCard® Credit Card account.

If it is after 6pm ET and you need your payment credited to your account today, you may call 1-800-520-1573 (TDD/TTY: 1-888-819-1918) during Customer Care Hours and speak to a Customer Care representative. Your payment must be received before 11:59 pm ET to be credited as a same-day payment. A fee may apply to same-day payments made with a representative.

What is an authorized buyer?

An authorized buyer is an individual you permit to make purchases on your account. You may designate up to 4 authorized buyers. As the primary account holder, you are responsible for any balance on this account. If you would like to add an authorized buyer to your account, please sign into your Account Center and click on Manage Authorized Buyers.
